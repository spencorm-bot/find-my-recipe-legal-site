Privacy Policy — Find My Recipe

Effective date: 2025-08-17

Find My Recipe (“we”, “us”, or “our”) lets you sign in with Facebook/Instagram and save recipes from posts you authorize.

Information we collect

  • Account info from login: name, email, profile picture, and user ID as provided by Facebook/Instagram.
  • Content you authorize: post/media metadata and captions needed to extract recipes.
  • App data: saved recipes, ratings, notes, basic analytics (device/browser).

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and link the Facebook/Instagram accounts you choose.
  • Import content you explicitly authorize and extract recipes for your personal use.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the app.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share with service providers only to run the app (hosting, databases).

Retention

We keep your data until you delete it in the app or request deletion.

Your choices

  • Disconnect social accounts at any time.
  • Delete your account/data (see Data Deletion).

Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

Contact

Email: you@example.com

Updates

We may update this policy. We’ll change the “Effective date” above when we do.
